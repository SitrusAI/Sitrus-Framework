Starter Suggestions

  1. Base Content: Set logo and boilerplate info in cms/general.json
  2. New Pages: Duplicate and rename /pages/page-template.html, and enable its navigation in app.json
  3. Theme: Modify theme values in styles/theme.css
  4. Header/Footer: Tweak or replace pages/components/header.html and footer.html
  5. Favicon: In the root folder, overwrite favicon.ico with your own for browser tabs.
  6. Replace this home page code.
